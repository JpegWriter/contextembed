# The Problem We Solve

## Problem 1: Invisible Images
Professional photographers shoot thousands of images, but most never get proper metadata. Without metadata:
- **Google Image Search** can't index them properly
- **AI search engines** (ChatGPT, Perplexity) can't reference them
- **Stock platforms** reject or bury them
- **Clients** can't find their own photos
- **Revenue is lost** to undiscoverable work

## Problem 2: Trust Erosion
AI-generated images are flooding the market. For service businesses (contractors, medical, legal), using fake "proof" images destroys credibility. Potential customers can't tell real work from AI fabrications.

## Problem 3: Legal Exposure
Images without proper copyright, credits, and usage terms leave photographers vulnerable to:
- Unauthorized usage without compensation
- Misattribution in AI training datasets
- Lost licensing revenue
